Glacier is one of the key objects of investigation and monitoring of natural resources in Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. In this paper, the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau is taken as the research area, and glaciers are taken as research objects. The glacier data of the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau in 2013 and 2017 are taken as samples. A model based on the random forest algorithm (R<sup>2</sup>=0.72) was constructed by machine learning, and the dataset of 1km-scale glaciers in Qinghai-Tibet Plateau from 2000 to 2020 were obtained. The results show that the area of glacier in the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau has decreased from 53377km<sup>2</sup> to 49615km<sup>2</sup> since 2000, with a decrease of 7.05%. Temperature and precipitation are the main factors affecting the trend and rate of glaciers change, and the local topographic differences are the main factors causing the abnormal glaciers change.
